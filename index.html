About Me

Michelle has been programming since last week, but she is wholeheartedly diving into the industry. After spending five years working at nonprofits in the Seattle area, mostly in marketing and communications, she decided to take her website game to the next level by joining Code Fellows. In the future, she hopes to be a Junior Developer.

In her limited spare time, Michelle can be found crafting, performing one-woman shows off-Broadway, singing opera, going to Aqua Zumba classes, reading The Evergrey, and devouring the local library's section of science fiction.
